How It Works

Find quality headhunters and avoid the sales pitches

Wrek uses data science to help you identify top recruiters.

  • Start by posting a job. Tell us about your vacancy and the candidate skills required.
  • Wrek analyses your needs. Our algorithms highlight your vacancy to selected recruiters based on their skills.
  • Let the bidding begin. Recruiters who are confident they can fill your vacancy submit bids.

Know who you're working with

Use our data to ensure you're working with professionals.

  • Browse profiles. View recruiters' profiles to see client ratings, placement success ratios, and reviews. Be confident that they have filled similar roles before.
  • Review bids. Evaluate proposed fees, taking into account each recruiter's track record.
  • Select your favourite. Choose your new recruitment partner and they'll commence the search.

Only pay the market price

A fixed fee is agreed up front, which is only payable if the vacancy is filled by a recruiter's candidate. This removes any alterior motive for recruiters to negotiate on salary.

  • Pay the market price. The bidding system ensures that you pay the market rate - not every role is worth a 20% fee.
  • Simplified payments. All of your recruitment fees are paid through Wrek.
  • Payment 30 days on start date. The payment mechanism remains the same - the fee is payable once a candidate has started. We'll also handle rebates for you if a candidate doesn't work out.
