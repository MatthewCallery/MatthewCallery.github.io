Recruitment Industry - Our View

We're tired of hearing that tech is going to kill the recruitment industry. It's a vital part of the job market and we want to restore its image with your help.

Our aim is to bring transparency to the sector, so that employers can easily identify professional headhunters and avoid those who employ the aggressive sales tactics that give recruitment a bad reputation.

Find new clients

Wrek makes it easy to connect with new clients, so you can focus on speaking to candidates.

  • Streamlined vacancy search. Wrek's algorithms highlight vacancies which match your expertise.
  • Client invitations. Build a compelling profile, and clients and candidates will begin to seek out your expertise.

Build your profile

Ever heard the words "That's what every recruiter promises" when pitching a potential client? Wrek gives you a verified online portfolio showcasing your successes, so you don't have to convince clients of your expertise.

  • Automatic placement tracking. Wrek updates your profile with each placement you make as proof of your success.
  • Client rating. Clients are invited to submit a rating for every vacancy you work on. This is your opportunity to showcase the quality of your work.
  • Enjoy the snowball effect. The greater the success you have filling vacancies, the more likely you are to get hired by clients using Wrek.

Get paid

Wrek minimises your need for back-office support by handling invoices and payments.

  • Placement fee. This is agreed with the client before the search commences.
  • All invoices and payments happen through Wrek. We generate invoices and chase payments on your behalf, allowing you to focus on recruitment.
  • Payment schedule. Payment is due within 30 days of the candidate's start date.

Service fees for recruiters

Wrek charges recruiters a sliding fee based on their lifetime billings with a client. The more business a recruiter does with a client, the more they'll earn.

  • 20% for the first £5,000 billed with the client.
  • 10% for lifetime billings with the client between £5,000 and £20,000.
  • 5% for lifetime billings with the client that exceed £20,000.
